Research

I am a Lecturer at the Department of Chemical Engineering, University of Bath. My research interests focus on two main areas: catalyst design and reactor engineering. In particular, the development of microreactors and new hybrid photo-electrocatalysts that can be applied in environmental processes such as artificial photosynthesis.

Previously I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Cambridge in the Catalysis and Process Integration Group. Before that I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Warwick, and a Research Scientist at Stoli Catalysts. I obtained my PhD from the Universidad de Castilla-La Mancha where I also studied as an undergraduate.
